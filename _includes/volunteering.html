Community Service

I’m deeply committed to volunteering my time to mentor and support the personal and professional growth of youth in my community. In the summer of 2024, I independently took on a passion project to author and present a comprehensive 5-part Career Development curriculum covering everything I wish I had known as a student, along with the insights I’ve gained throughout my career. It’s been an incredibly rewarding experience sharing this knowledge with dozens of high school-aged participants through impactful nonprofit organizations such as Girl Magic Inc., Crittenton of NC, and Carolina Youth Coalition, with the hope of inspiring and supporting the next generation. You can access the full curriculum below and share it with whoever may benefit.

Community Involvement

I am also passionate about engaging with my community through ad-hoc volunteering with organizations such as Feeding Charlotte and Second Harvest Food Bank of Metrolina, as well as serving on various committees for organizations. I enjoy connecting with like-minded individuals who share my commitment to giving back, personal development, and other common interests. Collaborating with others who prioritize community impact and development is always an energizing experience. My involvement includes serving in a leadership capacity on the following committees:


First Gen Success

Finance Committee (Spring 2024 - Present)

First Gen Success is a cause that is personal to me, as I remember the unique challenges I faced as a first-generation student. I love seeing the students being supported, uplifted, and encouraged to reach new heights.


Charlotte Young Professionals Group

Digital Development Committee (Fall 2024 - Present)

The Charlotte Young Professionals Group has been an incredible networking and professional development organization that has facilitated meaningful relationships with like-minded peers. It has also provided valuable opportunities for my personal and career growth, as well as new experiences.
