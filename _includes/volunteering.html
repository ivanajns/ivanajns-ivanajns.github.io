Volunteering

I’m deeply committed to volunteering my time to mentor and support the personal and professional growth of youth in my community. For 2024, I decided to take on a passion project by authoring and presenting a comprehensive 5-part Career Development curriculum that covers everything I wish I had known as a student, as well as the insights I’ve gained throughout my career. It’s been an incredible experience sharing this knowledge with dozens of high school-aged participants through impactful nonprofit organizations such as Girl's Inc., Crittenton of NC, and Big Brothers Big Sisters, with the hope of making a meaningful impact on the next generation.
