Volunteering

I’m deeply committed to volunteering my time to mentor and support the personal and professional growth of youth in my community. For 2024, I decided to take on a passion project where I authored and presented a comprehensive 5-part Career Development curriculum that covers everything I wish I had known as a student, as well as the insights I’ve gained throughout my career. It’s been an incredible experience sharing this knowledge with dozens of high school-aged participants through impactful nonprofit organizations such as Girl Magic Inc., Crittenton of NC, and Big Brothers Big Sisters, with the hope of making a meaningful impact on the next generation. Community

I am also passionate about engaging with my community through ad-hoc volunteering with organizations such as Feeding Charlotte and Second Harvest Food Bank of Metrolina, as well as serving on various committees for organizations. I enjoy connecting with like-minded individuals who share my commitment to giving back, personal development, and other shared interests and values. My involvement includes serving in a leadership capacity on the following committees:


First Gen Success - Finance Committee (Spring 2024 - Present)

First-generation success is a cause that is personal to me, as I remember the unique challenges I faced as a first-generation student. I love seeing the students being supported, uplifted, and encouraged to reach new heights.


Charlotte Young Professionals Group - Digital Development Committee (Fall 2024 - Present)

The Charlotte Young Professionals Group has been an incredible networking and professional development organization that has facilitated meaningful relationships with like-minded peers. It has also provided valuable opportunities for my personal and career growth, as well as new experiences.


Compass Group - Social Committee (Fall 2024 - Present)

I serve on the social committee at work because I believe in the importance of fostering an enjoyable work environment that allows coworkers to connect and build strong relationships across our teams.
